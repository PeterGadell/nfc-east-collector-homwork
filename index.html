NFC East Collector Game

Rules of the Gridiron

A random number will be generated for you at the start of the game.

There are four NFC East teams to the right of your screen:

  1. Cowboys
  2. Giants
  3. Eagles
  4. Redskins

Clicking on a team will add a certain amount of points to your total score on the Scoreboard.

To win the game, your total score must be equal to the random number

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each team is hidden from you until you click on it.

Every new game will generate new values for each team.
